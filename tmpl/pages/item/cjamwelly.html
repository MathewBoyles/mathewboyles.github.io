Adobe Creative Jam

The Adobe Creative Jam is a competition in which teams take part to create a design from a specified brief. The New Zealand jams are organised by Adobe and Design Assembly.

Tim - a friend in my Web & UX design course - and I, applied for the Wellington competition for 2017 and were excited when we were accepted to take part.

The Brief

The design brief for 2017 was to create a website or other digital campaign to encourage young people (aged 18-24) to vote in the upcoming New Zealand elections.

Our Concept

Patrick Gower is a well-known public figure in New Zealand journalism. In 2014, he was featured in a video which quickly went viral on social media, making him popular with young people. For this reason, we wanted to use him as our main focus for this design.

#TakeChargeNZ refers to young people taking power to push the future in the direction they want, and the tagline "GIVE A F**K THIS ELECTION" was used as it also evoked the passion for driving forward. The tagline relates to the popular video.
