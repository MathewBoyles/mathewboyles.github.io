Lightfarm Design Studio

The re-design of the Lightfarm website was created as part of a marked UI/UX course assessment. It was not created in association with Lightfarm.

We were tasked with creating a website for the purpose of displaying content pulled from the Behance API. Omri and I decided to us Lightfarm as our brand for this project as we were intrigued by their style and thought we could create a relevant website and colour scheme to match.

The Brief

The design firm you have begun working for has given your team a task of creating an app to show off the work of their designers. This work will be hosted on Behance so the app must consume data from the Behance API.
The firm wants a single page application (SPA) which integrates nicely into their existing site.
Using the Behance API, the app should allow the user to:
  • view a list of designers
  • view a designer’s details
  • view a designer’s list of projects
  • view a project’s details
  • visualise the overall statistics of a designer’s projects, including the number of views, appreciations, and comments
